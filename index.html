Ultrasonic Glass for blind

Ultrasonic Glass for Blind is a smart assistive device designed to help visually impaired individuals navigate safely. It uses ultrasonic sensors to detect obstacles in front of the user and measures their distance. When an obstacle is detected within a certain range, the system provides alerts through a buzzer or vibration motor, enabling the user to avoid collisions. The device is lightweight, cost-effective, and enhances independent mobility and safety for visually impaired people.
